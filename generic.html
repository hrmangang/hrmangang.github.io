About

I am a math undergrad at IISER Mohali. I am mostly into analysis and geometry. I am also interested in theoretical computer science, especially algorithms and computational complexity.

This is my site which I developed during my first major year at college. I plan to upload my scientific and non-scientific articles here plus blogs and notes which I think might help fellow math learners. Besides all that, I might put some cheat sheets (because I love them!) and maybe links and recommedations for learning math or computing. I shall keep updating this site and I hope it helps people who visit here.

If you like the things here or have criticisms, please drop a message or mail. I shall be happy to know your thoughts and opinions.
